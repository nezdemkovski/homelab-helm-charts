1. Cloudflare Tunnel has been deployed successfully!

2. Check the tunnel status:
   kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "cloudflared.name" . }},app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}"

3. View tunnel logs:
   kubectl logs -f deployment/{{ include "cloudflared.fullname" . }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

4. Check tunnel health:
   kubectl port-forward deployment/{{ include "cloudflared.fullname" . }} 2000:2000 -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}
   curl http://localhost:2000/ready

5. Your applications should now be accessible through Cloudflare Tunnel at your configured domain.

For more information, visit: https://developers.cloudflare.com/cloudflare-one/connections/connect-apps/
